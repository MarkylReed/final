I was born in Okinawa-ken, Japan.
My mother was a high school teacher, and my dad was missionary.
After they got married and had me, they let me stay in Japan for half of my formative years. They wanted to give me the chance to choose if I wanted to go back later in life.
We moved to the states when I was ten or eleven- I can't remember anymore.
It was fine, I even ended up making a really good friend. I didn't have many friends in Okinawa.
I didn't have any friends in Okinawa.
Eventually, my mom and dad got divorced. I have a half sister now, but I've only met her once or twice.
I don't remember much of my life, in all honesty.
